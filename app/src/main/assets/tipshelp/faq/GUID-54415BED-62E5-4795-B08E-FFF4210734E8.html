If I sign in with my Sina Weibo account, will HTC have access to any of my personal information?

Yes. After you sign in with your Sina Weibo account, HTC will have access to your Sina Weibo user ID, email address or phone number, gender, name, country and the language you use. If you are not comfortable with this, then please do not sign in with your Sina Weibo account on the HTC Account screen. And at all points, you will be able to control whether or not HTC reaches out to you periodically by email with HTC offers, product information, and newsletters.
