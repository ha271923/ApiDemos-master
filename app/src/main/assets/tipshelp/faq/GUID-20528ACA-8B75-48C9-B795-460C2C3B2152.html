What other HTC services can I access with my HTC Account?

Starting from the 2013 release of the HTC One, you’ll be able to use your HTC Account to access all HTC services that are available in your region, including HTC Zoe™ Share, HTC Get Started, and HTC Backup. If you purchased your phone in China, other than those services mentioned above, you will also be able to use your HTC Account to access Game Zone and other HTC services in the near future.

We’re constantly working on new services and expanding the availability for existing ones, and will make them available to HTC Account users when they are ready for launch.
