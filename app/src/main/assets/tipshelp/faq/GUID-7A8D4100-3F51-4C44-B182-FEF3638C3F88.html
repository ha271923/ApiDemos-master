I forgot my HTC Account password. Can I reset it?

Yes.
  1. On your computer, open your web browser and go to our website (www.htcsense.com).
  2. On the upper-right corner, click Sign in > Sign in with HTC Account, and then click the Forgot your password link in the drop-down box.
  3. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
Alternatively, go to Settings > Accounts & sync on your phone. Tap Add > HTC Account > Sign in with my HTC Account > Reset password, and follow the instruction to reset your password.
