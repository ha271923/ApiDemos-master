How do I find the IMEI/MEID of my phone?

To find the IMEI/IMEID of your phone, you can check one of the following:
  • Use two fingers to slide the Quick settings panel open and tap Settings > About phone > Phone identity.
  • On the phone keypad, dial *#06#; this will automatically display the phone's IMEI.
