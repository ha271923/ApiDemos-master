How do I exit/quit the Car app? Pressing the Home button doesn’t seem to work.

For phones that use HTC Sense 5.0, an ​“‍Exit” button is added to the Car interface. (There was no ​“‍Exit” onscreen button on phones with Sense 4+ or earlier version). You can tap ​“‍Exit” on the screen or press the Back button to exit/quit the Car app.
