I have been using an HTC Watch account. Can I use it as my HTC Account?

Yes, you can use your HTC Watch account to sign in to your HTC Account on HTC phones (HTC One series and later phones only). But, you won’t be able to use your HTC Watch account to sign in to our website and access our web services. You’ll need to create a new HTC Account using a different email address to access our website.
