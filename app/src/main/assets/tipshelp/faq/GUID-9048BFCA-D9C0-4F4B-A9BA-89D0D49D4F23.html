What would happen if I don’t verify my email address?

Starting from the HTC One X series (launched in the first half of 2012), new devices will include a 90-day grace period when you sign in/up for the very first time. In that grace period, you can use your HTC Account and the HTC services that rely on it for authentication.

You will receive a notification message to remind you to verify your email address every 5 days within the grace period. After the grace period, whenever you need to access any service, for example, HTC Backup on newer HTC phones, you will be asked to verify your email address. Follow the instruction on the message you see to verify your email address.
