Does having an HTC Account mean that you’ll spam me with emails?

No. You’ll be asked whether to receive promotion email when you create an HTC Account or when you first sign in to your Facebook or Sina Weibo account.
Note: You will have to verify your email address before editing your profile on our website.
  1. Go to http://www.htcsense.com and sign in to your HTC account.
  2. Click the drop-down arrow at the upper-right corner.
  3. Click Account settings > Edit to subscribe or unsubscribe.
  4. Click Save to save the changes.
