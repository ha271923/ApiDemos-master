Screen not rotating?

Recalibrate the screen if you feel that the screen orientation does not respond properly to the way you hold [generic phone name].
  1. Slide the Notifications panel open, and then tap .
  2. Tap Display, gestures & buttons (or Display & buttons) and then tap G-Sensor calibration.
  3. Place [generic phone name] on a flat and level surface, and then tap Calibrate.
  4. After the recalibration process, tap OK.
