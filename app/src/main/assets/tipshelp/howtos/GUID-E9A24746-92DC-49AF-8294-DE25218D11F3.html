Sharing the link to your Dropbox

Share the link to your entire album of photos and videos that's in your Dropbox so other people can access it.
  1. From the Gallery Albums screen, tap Dropbox.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • Tap an album to share, and then tap Share.
    • To share a single photo, tap it to display it, then tap it again and tap Share.
  3. Choose how you want to share the link to this album or photo, such as sending it by email or posting it to your social networks.
Tip: You can also use the Dropbox app. Tap that appears next to the album or file, and then tap Share.
