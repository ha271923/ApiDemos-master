Uploading your photos and videos to Dropbox from Gallery

  1. Open the Gallery app.
  2. Tap My photos, and then tap the album that contains the photos or videos you want to share.
  3. Tap Share > Dropbox.
  4. Select the photos or videos you want to upload, and then tap Next.
  5. Create or choose a folder in Dropbox, and then tap Upload.
Tip:
To view your uploads right from Gallery, put them under the Photos folder in your Dropbox. Or under Photos, create a folder and upload them to this folder.
