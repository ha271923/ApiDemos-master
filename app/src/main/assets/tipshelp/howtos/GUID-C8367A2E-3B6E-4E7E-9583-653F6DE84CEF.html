Setting up Google Now

Important: Before setting up Google Now:
  • Make sure that you're signed in to your Google account.
  • Turn location services on and make sure that you have an Internet connection.
  1. Open the Google app. Or press and hold .
  2. The subsequent screens show examples of Google Now information cards. Tap Next until you reach the Get Google Now! screen.
  3. Tap Yes, I'm in. Google Now information cards will start to appear on the Google search screen.
  4. To see all available information cards, tap Show sample cards.
More information cards will appear on the Google search screen as you use [generic phone name] to search the Web, create appointments, and more.
