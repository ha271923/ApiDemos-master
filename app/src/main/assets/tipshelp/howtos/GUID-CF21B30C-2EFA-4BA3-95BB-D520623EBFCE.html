Unlocking the screen

You can:
  • Drag the lock icon or clock up.
  • Swipe up from an empty space on the lock screen.
If you've set up a screen lock, you'll be asked to provide your credentials before [generic phone name] unlocks.
Tip: If you've set an event reminder or an alarm, you can snooze or dismiss the event or alarm right from the lock screen. Just drag or up.
