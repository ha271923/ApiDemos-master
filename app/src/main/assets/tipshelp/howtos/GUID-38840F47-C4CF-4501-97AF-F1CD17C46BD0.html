Your contacts list

The People app lists all contacts you've stored on [generic phone name] and from online accounts you're logged in to. Use the People app to easily manage communications with people that matter to you.
  1. Open the People app.
  2. On your contacts list, you can:
    • View your profile and edit your contact information.
    • Create, edit, find, or send contacts.
    • See status updates.
    • Tap a contact photo to find ways to quickly connect with the contact.
    • See a notification icon when a contact has sent you new messages.
    • Check out who's online in Google Talk™. Online status icons are displayed if you’re signed in to Google Talk.
Tip: To sort your contacts by their first or last name, tap > Settings > Sort contact list.
