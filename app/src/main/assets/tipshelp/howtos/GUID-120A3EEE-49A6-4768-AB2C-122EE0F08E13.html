Setting search options

  1. Open the Google app. Or press and hold .
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the screen, and then tap > Settings to set the following options:
    • Voice. Set the Google Voice Search language, speech output, and more.
    • Phone search. Select which apps on [generic phone name] is included when doing a search.
    • Privacy & accounts. Manage your location history, enable recent web searches to show, or clear your search history.
