Taking a panoramic photo

Whether it’s the Golden Gate Bridge or the Eiffel Tower, you can capture anything wide or tall in a single photo.
Important: Some camera features are not available when you’re in this mode, such as zooming.
  1. On the Viewfinder screen, tap .
  2. Under Photo capture mode, tap Sweep panorama.
  3. When you're ready to take the shot, tap . Directional arrows appear, prompting you to pan left or right in landscape view (or pan up or down in portrait view).
  4. Pan [generic phone name] as smoothly as you can to automatically capture frames.

    You can also tap to stop capturing anytime.

The camera stitches the frames into a single photo.
