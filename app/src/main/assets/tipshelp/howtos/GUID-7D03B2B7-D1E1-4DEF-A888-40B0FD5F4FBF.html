Downloading offline maps

Do you frequently visit a specific area or place? Download an offline map so you can quickly access that map and save on bandwidth too.
Important: Offline maps may not be available in all countries or regions.
  1. Open the Maps app.
  2. Tap > Make available offline.
  3. Move the portion of the map you want to download within the cropping square.
    Tip: To reduce the size of the map, zoom in to get only the portion of the map you need.
  4. Tap Done.
