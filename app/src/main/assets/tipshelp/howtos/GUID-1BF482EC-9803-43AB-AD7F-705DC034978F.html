Protecting [generic phone name] with a screen lock

Protect your personal information and prevent others from using [generic phone name] without your permission by setting a screen lock pattern, face unlock, PIN, or password.

You'll be asked to unlock the screen every time [generic phone name] is turned on or when it’s idle for a certain period of time.

  1. Slide the Notifications panel open, and then tap > Security.
  2. Tap Screen lock.
  3. Select a screen lock option and set up the screen lock.
  4. Tap Lock phone after, then specify the idle time before the screen is locked.
    Tip: You can also clear Make pattern visible or Make passwords visible if you don’t want your screen lock to display as you enter it onscreen.
