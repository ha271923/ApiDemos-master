Choosing which calendars to show

  1. In any calendar view, tap > Accounts (Multi-calendar).
  2. Select or clear an account you wish to show or hide.

    If you have multiple calendars under an online account, tap to select the items to include.

Note:
  • Calendars are synced on [generic phone name], even if they are hidden.
  • You can also show or hide tasks that you've created on [generic phone name].
