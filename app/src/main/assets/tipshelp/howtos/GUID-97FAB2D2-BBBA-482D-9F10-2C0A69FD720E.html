Updating album covers and artist photos

Important: To save on data usage, you might want to connect to a Wi-Fi network when updating your album covers and artist photos. In Music settings, select Wi-Fi only.
  1. Open the Music app.
  2. Tap , and then select Artists or Albums.
  3. Tap > Update artist photos or Update album art.
If you want [generic phone name] to automatically check and update your music collection, tap on the Music app's main screen, and then tap Settings. Select Album art and Artist photos.
