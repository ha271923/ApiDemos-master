Syncing playlists between your phone and your computer

Set HTC Sync Manager to sync iTunes and Windows Media Player playlists between your phone and your computer.
  1. Connect your phone to your computer.
  2. Click Music > SETTINGS, and then select Sync selected playlist.
  3. Select the names of playlists that you want to sync.
  4. Click Apply (Windows only).
  5. Click if this is your first time to sync. To allow HTC Sync Manager to auto sync your playlists and other items (such as documents, media files, and data) next time, click More > SYNC SETTINGS, and then select Sync automatically whenever the phone connects.
