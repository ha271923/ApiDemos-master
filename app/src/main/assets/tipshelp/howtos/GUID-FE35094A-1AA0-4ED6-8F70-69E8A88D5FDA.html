Syncing notes

You can choose whether to automatically or manually sync your notes between [generic phone name] and your Evernote account on the Web.
Note: You need to log in to your Evernote account from [generic phone name] to start syncing notes.
  1. Open the Notes app.
  2. To manually sync notes, tap , and then tap Sync.
  3. To set Notes to automatically update, tap on the All notes screen, and then tap Settings > Accounts & sync.
  4. Tap your Evernote account, and then customize your sync settings.
