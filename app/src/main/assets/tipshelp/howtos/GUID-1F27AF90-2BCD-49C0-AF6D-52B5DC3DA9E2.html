Exploring what's around you

Feeling adventurous? With just a few taps, you can find interesting places around you quickly.
  1. In HTC Car, tap Navigation.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • Tap a category.
    • Tap to enter your own search keywords.
Tip: You can also find places by tapping Speak on the main screen and saying what you are looking for. For example say "‍Find parking"‍.
