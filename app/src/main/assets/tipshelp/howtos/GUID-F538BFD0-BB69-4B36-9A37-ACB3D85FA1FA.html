Syncing your accounts

  1. Slide the Notifications panel open, and then tap > Accounts & sync.
  2. You can:
    • Tap the Auto sync On/Off switch to turn automatic sync of all your accounts on or off.
    • Manually sync individual accounts. Tap an account type (such as for Exchange ActiveSyncÂ®), and then tap the account you want to sync. On the Account settings screen, tap > Sync now.
Tip: On the Account settings screen, you can also change the sync settings for an account.
