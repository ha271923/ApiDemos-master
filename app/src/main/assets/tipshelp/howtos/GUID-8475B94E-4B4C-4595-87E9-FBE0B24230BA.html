Auto uploading camera shots

Your new camera shots will be uploaded automatically, if you turned on the Camera Upload feature when you set up your Dropbox account on [generic phone name]. If you didn't turn it on before, you can enable it in the Dropbox settings.
  1. Open the Dropbox app.
  2. Tap > Settings > Turn On Camera Upload.
  3. Choose the type of Internet connection to use for uploading your captured photos and videos to Dropbox.
  4. Tap Turn on.
