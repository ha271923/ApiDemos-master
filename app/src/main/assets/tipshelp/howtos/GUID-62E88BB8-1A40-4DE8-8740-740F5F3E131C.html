Using [generic phone name] as a wireless router

Share your data connection with other devices by turning [generic phone name] into a wireless router.

Note:
  • Make sure the data connection is turned on.
  • You must have an approved data plan associated with your account from your mobile service provider to use this service.
  1. Slide the Notifications panel open, and then tap .
  2. Under Wireless & networks, tap More.
  3. Tap Mobile network sharing > Portable Wi-Fi hotspot settings.
  4. Enter a router name or use the default router name.
  5. Choose the type of security and set the password (key) for your wireless router. If you selected None in Security, you donâ€™t need to enter a password.
    Note: The password is the key other people need to enter on their device so they can connect and use [generic phone name] as a wireless router.
  6. Select the Portable Wi-Fi Hotspot option to turn on the wireless router.

[generic phone name] is ready to be used as a wireless router when you see on the status bar.
