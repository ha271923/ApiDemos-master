Downloading apps from the web

You can download apps directly from websites.
Important: Apps downloaded from websites can be from unknown sources. To protect [generic phone name] and your personal data, we strongly recommend that you only download from websites you trust.
  1. Open your web browser, then go to the website where you can download the app you want.
  2. Follow the website's download instructions for the app.
Before you can install the downloaded app, make sure that you've set [generic phone name] to allow installation of third-party apps in Settings > Security > Unknown sources.
