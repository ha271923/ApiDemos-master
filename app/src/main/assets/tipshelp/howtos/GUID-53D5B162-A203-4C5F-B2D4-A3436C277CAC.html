How can I allow my child to access an app in Kid Mode?

When in Kid Mode, you can choose which apps on [generic phone name] your child will have access to.
  1. On the Zoodles main screen, tap Parent Dashboard.
    Note: You can also go to Parent Dashboard from Settings > Security > Kid Mode.
  2. Confirm your action with your child lock setting (either by drawing the letter Z on the screen or by entering the child's birth year).
  3. Under Basic Features, tap Apps. You'll see a list of apps installed on [generic phone name].
  4. Tap the check option beside the app that you want your child to be able to use in Zoodles.
Tip: Want to receive or block incoming calls while in Kid Mode? Under Basic Features, tap Settings and then select or clear the Allow incoming calls check option.
