Dismissing or snoozing event reminders

If you have set at least one reminder for an event, the upcoming event icon will appear in the notifications area of the status bar to remind you.
  1. Open the Notifications panel.
  2. Spread your two fingers on the event notification to expand it, and then do one of the following:
    • Choose to snooze or dismiss all event reminders. Or, to quickly dismiss event notifications in the list, drag it left or right.
    • For a notification with multiple events, tap it to see the event list.
    • If you've invited guests, tap Send mail to send a quick response to the meeting attendees.
    • Press to keep the reminders pending in the notifications area of the status bar.
Note: To add messages or edit your list of quick responses, open Calendar, and then tap > Settings > Quick response.
