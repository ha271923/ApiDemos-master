Finding places in HTC Car

  1. In HTC Car, tap Navigation.
  2. Do one of the following:
    Search for a new place
    1. Tap .
    2. Type what you are looking for, and tap .
    Look up a previously searched place Tap Previous.
    Get directions for an upcoming event in Calendar
    1. Tap Appointments.
    2. Tap an upcoming appointment from the list.
  3. Tap an item to see the place on the map.
  4. Tap Start to get the directions.
