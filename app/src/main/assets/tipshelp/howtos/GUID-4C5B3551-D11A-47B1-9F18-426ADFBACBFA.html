Searching the Web with your voice

Use Google Voice Search to find information on the Web just by speaking to [generic phone name].
  1. Open the Google app. Or press and hold .
  2. Say "‍Google"‍, and then say what you want to search for. After speaking, matching items from Google web search are shown.
  3. If what you’re searching for is in the list, tap the item to open it in its compatible app. Otherwise, tap to search again.
