Editing a group shot

Finding it difficult to choose a group shot with the most smiles and the fewest blinks? Retouch a group shot so everyone looks their best in the photo.
Important: You can use this feature when editing HTC Zoe photos. This feature is only available for continuous shots that are saved as a batch.
  1. Tap the album that has the photos or videos you want to share.
  2. Browse to the photo that you want to edit.
  3. Tap the photo, and then tap Edit > Retouch > Always Smile. You'll see circles around any faces detected in the photo.
  4. Select one face at a time and drag it sideways with your finger to select the expression you want.
  5. After making your changes, tap Done.
  6. Tap > Save. The edited photo is saved as a copy. The original photo remains unedited.
