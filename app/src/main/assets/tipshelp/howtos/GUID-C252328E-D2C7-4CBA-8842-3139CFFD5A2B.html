Using voice commands in HTC Car

Don't feel like poking around on your screen while you're driving? Use your voice to control HTC Car.
  1. In HTC Car, tap the screen with three fingers to activate the voice command mode.
    Tip: You can also tap Speak on the main screen in HTC Car.
  2. Do one of the following:
    Call someone Say "‍Call [person's name in phonebook]"‍
    Play music Say "‍Play [song title, album, artist name, or genre]"‍
    Play the radio Say "‍Listen to [song title, artist name, or genre]"‍
    Find something on the map Say "‍Find [name of place, address, type of place]"‍
