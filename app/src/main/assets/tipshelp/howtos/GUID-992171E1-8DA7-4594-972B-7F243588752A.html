Restoring a SIM card that has been locked out

If you enter the wrong PIN more times than the maximum number of attempts allowed, your SIM card will become ​“‍PUK-locked.”

You need a PUK code to restore access to [generic phone name]. Contact your mobile operator for this code.
  1. On the Phone dialer screen, enter the PUK code, and then tap Next.
  2. Enter the new PIN you want to use, and then tap Next.
  3. Enter the new PIN again, and then tap OK.
